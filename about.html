About Westley Resource

Building bridges between exceptional IT talent and innovative companies since 2011.

Our Story

Founded in 2011, Westley Resource emerged from a simple observation: the technology industry needed a recruiting partner that truly understood both the technical landscape and the human element of hiring.

What started as a small team of passionate recruiters has grown into a premier IT staffing firm, but our core mission remains unchanged: connecting the right people with the right opportunities.

Today, we're proud to serve hundreds of companies across various industries, from innovative startups to Fortune 500 enterprises, while helping thousands of IT professionals advance their careers.

Our Mission

To empower organizations and IT professionals by creating meaningful connections that drive innovation, growth, and career success.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and respected IT recruiting partner, known for our integrity, expertise, and commitment to excellence.

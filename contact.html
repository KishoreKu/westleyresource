Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you fill a position?

Our average time-to-hire is 14 days, though this can vary based on role complexity and requirements. We typically present qualified candidates within 3-5 business days of our initial consultation.

What are your fees?

We offer flexible pricing models including contingency (20-25% of first-year salary), retained search, and volume hiring discounts. Contact us for a customized quote based on your specific needs.

Do candidates pay any fees?

No, our services are completely free for candidates. We're compensated by employers when we make successful placements.

What is your placement guarantee?

We offer a 90-day replacement guarantee. If a placement doesn't work out within the first 90 days, we'll find you another qualified candidate at no additional cost.

What industries do you serve?

We work with companies across all industries including fintech, healthcare, e-commerce, SaaS, cybersecurity, and more. Our focus is on IT roles regardless of industry vertical.
